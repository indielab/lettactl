# Common Ingredient Substitutions

## Dairy Substitutions
- **Heavy cream**: Half-and-half + butter, or milk + butter
- **Buttermilk**: Regular milk + lemon juice or vinegar (let sit 5 minutes)
- **Sour cream**: Greek yogurt, or cream cheese + lemon juice
- **Butter**: Oil (use 3/4 amount), or applesauce for baking

## Baking Substitutions
- **1 egg**: 1/4 cup applesauce, 1 mashed banana, or flax egg
- **Baking powder**: Baking soda + cream of tartar (1:2 ratio)
- **Brown sugar**: White sugar + molasses
- **Self-rising flour**: All-purpose flour + baking powder + salt

## Pantry Staples
- **Garlic**: Garlic powder (1/8 teaspoon = 1 clove)
- **Onion**: Onion powder (1 tablespoon = 1 medium onion)
- **Fresh herbs**: Dried herbs (use 1/3 amount)
- **Wine**: Broth, grape juice, or vinegar + water

## Proteins
- **Ground beef**: Ground turkey, chicken, or plant-based alternatives
- **Chicken stock**: Vegetable stock + soy sauce for umami
- **Anchovies**: Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, or miso paste

## Vegetables
- **Mushrooms**: Eggplant or zucchini for texture
- **Bell peppers**: Any color can substitute for another
- **Tomatoes**: Canned when fresh not available (adjust liquid in recipe)
- **Spinach**: Kale, Swiss chard, or other leafy greens

## Flavor Enhancers
- **Lemon juice**: Vinegar or lime juice
- **Vanilla extract**: Almond extract (use half amount)
- **Hot sauce**: Red pepper flakes + vinegar
- **Worcestershire**: Soy sauce + vinegar + sugar
