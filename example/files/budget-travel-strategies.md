# Budget Travel Strategies

## Accommodation Savings

### Budget-Friendly Options
- **Hostels**: Great for solo travelers and making connections
- **Airbnb**: Often cheaper for longer stays, especially with kitchen access
- **House-sitting**: Free accommodation in exchange for pet/house care
- **Home exchanges**: Swap homes with travelers from your destination
- **University dorms**: Available during summer breaks in many cities

### Booking Strategies
- Book early for popular destinations and peak seasons
- Use incognito browsing to avoid price tracking
- Compare prices across multiple booking platforms
- Consider accommodation slightly outside city centers
- Look for properties with kitchenettes to save on dining

## Transportation Savings

### Flight Deals
- Use flight comparison websites and set price alerts
- Be flexible with dates and nearby airports
- Consider budget airlines for short flights
- Book round-trip vs one-way for better rates
- Use airline miles and credit card rewards

### Local Transportation
- Research multi-day transit passes
- Walking and biking when weather permits
- Share rides and split costs
- Use public transport apps for real-time information
- Avoid airport transportation when alternatives exist

## Food & Dining

### Eating Like a Local
- Street food and local markets for authentic experiences
- Grocery shopping and cooking some meals
- Look for restaurants away from tourist areas
- Ask locals for their favorite affordable spots
- Take advantage of lunch specials and happy hours

### Money-Saving Tips
- Pack snacks for day trips and long travel days
- Share large portions with travel companions
- BYOB where culturally appropriate and legal
- Picnics in parks and scenic spots
- Local food tours often include multiple venues for one price

## Activities & Experiences

### Free and Low-Cost Activities
- Free walking tours (tip-based)
- Museums with free days or reduced-price hours
- Parks, beaches, and hiking trails
- Local festivals and cultural events
- Free concerts and outdoor performances

### Smart Spending
- City tourism cards that bundle attractions
- Student, senior, or group discounts
- Off-season pricing for activities
- Combo tickets for multiple attractions
- Local Groupon-style deals and promotions
