# Destination Research Tips

## Information Sources

### Official Resources
- Country/city tourism boards and official websites
- Embassy websites for entry requirements and safety
- Government travel advisories and health recommendations
- Official transportation authority websites

### Travel Communities
- Recent travel blogs and vlogs (within last 2 years)
- Travel forums: Reddit, TripAdvisor, Lonely Planet Thorn Tree
- Social media location tags and travel hashtags
- Local Facebook groups and travel communities

### Local Insights
- Local news websites to understand current events
- Local food blogs and restaurant review sites
- Cultural centers and museums for event calendars
- Local tour companies for unique experiences

## Key Research Areas

### Practical Information
- Visa requirements and processing times
- Currency, exchange rates, and payment methods
- Local SIM cards vs international roaming options
- Electrical outlets and adapter requirements
- Time zones and potential jet lag impact

### Cultural Context
- Religious customs and appropriate behavior
- Business hours and local holiday schedules
- Dress codes for different venues and occasions
- Tipping culture and service expectations
- Common greetings and polite phrases

### Safety Considerations
- Crime rates and common tourist scams
- Political climate and areas to avoid
- Health risks and recommended vaccinations
- Natural disaster risks and seasonal concerns
- Emergency contact numbers and services

## Budget Planning

### Cost Research
- Average daily costs for accommodation, food, transport
- Entrance fees for major attractions
- Local transportation costs and multi-day passes
- Restaurant price ranges from budget to splurge
- Shopping and souvenir budget considerations

### Money-Saving Strategies
- Free walking tours and museum days
- Local markets vs tourist restaurants
- Public transportation vs taxis/rideshares
- Shoulder season vs peak season pricing
- Group discounts and advance booking deals
