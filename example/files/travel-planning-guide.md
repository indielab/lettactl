# Comprehensive Travel Planning Guide

## Pre-Trip Planning (2-6 Months Ahead)

### Research & Decision Making
- Define trip goals and must-see experiences
- Research visa requirements and passport validity
- Check seasonal weather and local events
- Set realistic budget including contingency funds
- Book flights and accommodations early for better deals

### Health & Safety Preparation
- Check required vaccinations and health advisories
- Purchase travel insurance
- Research common scams and safety concerns
- Register with embassy for international travel
- Get international driving permits if needed

## Itinerary Development (1-2 Months Ahead)

### Activity Planning
- Balance must-see sights with relaxation time
- Research operating hours and advance booking requirements
- Consider travel time between locations
- Plan for jet lag and adjust first-day expectations
- Leave flexibility for spontaneous discoveries

### Practical Logistics
- Research local transportation options
- Download offline maps and translation apps
- Learn basic phrases in local language
- Research tipping customs and payment methods
- Check electrical adapters needed

## Packing Strategy (1-2 Weeks Ahead)

### Essential Documents
- Passport, visas, travel insurance papers
- Copies stored separately and digitally
- Emergency contact information
- Medical information and prescriptions
- Travel itinerary and confirmation numbers

### Smart Packing Tips
- Check airline baggage restrictions
- Pack versatile clothing that layers
- Bring comfortable walking shoes
- Pack essentials in carry-on bag
- Leave space for souvenirs

## During Travel

### Cultural Awareness
- Respect local customs and dress codes
- Learn proper greetings and basic etiquette
- Support local businesses and communities
- Be patient with language barriers
- Keep an open mind about different ways of life

### Staying Connected
- Inform banks of travel plans
- Set up international phone/data plans
- Share itinerary with trusted contacts
- Check in regularly with family/friends
- Keep emergency contacts readily available
