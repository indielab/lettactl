# Travel Preferences

## Travel Style
- Type: Mix of adventure and relaxation
- Pace: Moderate - like to see things but not rush
- Group: Usually travels with partner
- Accommodation: Mid-range hotels, occasional Airbnb

## Budget
- Daily budget: $150-200 per person
- Splurge areas: Good food, unique experiences
- Save on: Transportation, accommodation (within reason)
- Trip length: Usually 7-10 days

## Interests & Activities
- **Love**: Local food, historical sites, museums, walking tours
- **Enjoy**: Light hiking, cultural events, markets, scenic drives  
- **Avoid**: Extreme sports, party scenes, very crowded tourist traps
- **Must-have**: Good restaurants, comfortable walking shoes weather

## Practical Preferences
- Season: Prefer shoulder seasons (spring/fall)
- Climate: Mild temperatures, not too humid
- Transportation: Willing to fly, prefer not to rent cars in cities
- Language: English or places with good English support

## Past Favorites
- Italy (loved the food and history)
- Pacific Northwest (great scenery and cities)
- Japan (amazing culture and organization)
