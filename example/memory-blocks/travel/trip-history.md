# Trip History & Experiences

## Recent Trips

### Italy - Rome & Tuscany (Spring 2024)
- **Duration**: 10 days
- **Highlights**: Vatican Museums, Tuscan countryside, amazing pasta everywhere
- **What worked**: Booking tours in advance, staying in smaller towns
- **What didn't**: Too much time in Rome, not enough in countryside
- **Rating**: 9/10

### Pacific Northwest - Seattle & Portland (Summer 2023)
- **Duration**: 8 days
- **Highlights**: Pike Place Market, Columbia River Gorge, amazing coffee culture
- **What worked**: Perfect weather, great food scene, easy city navigation
- **What didn't**: Should have rented a car for more nature exploration
- **Rating**: 8/10

### Japan - Tokyo & Kyoto (Fall 2022)
- **Duration**: 12 days  
- **Highlights**: Temple visits, incredible food, efficient transportation
- **What worked**: JR Pass, staying in traditional ryokan, respectful cultural immersion
- **What didn't**: Language barrier was bigger challenge than expected
- **Rating**: 10/10

## Lessons Learned
- Always pack comfortable walking shoes
- Research local food customs beforehand
- Book popular restaurants/attractions early
- Leave flexibility for spontaneous discoveries
- Invest in good travel insurance
