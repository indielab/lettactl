# Destination Knowledge & Travel Tips

## Research Methods
- Use multiple sources: travel blogs, official tourism sites, recent reviews
- Check visa requirements and entry restrictions
- Research local customs and etiquette
- Look up seasonal weather patterns and events

## Booking Strategies
- **Flights**: Book 6-8 weeks in advance for domestic, 8-12 weeks for international
- **Hotels**: Compare prices across booking sites, read recent reviews
- **Activities**: Book popular attractions in advance, leave some time unplanned
- **Restaurants**: Make reservations for special places, but also leave room for discoveries

## Packing Wisdom
- Check airline baggage policies
- Pack versatile layers for weather changes
- Always pack essentials in carry-on
- Bring comfortable walking shoes and one dressier option

## Money & Safety
- Notify banks of travel plans
- Research local tipping customs
- Keep copies of important documents
- Research common tourist scams for destination

## Cultural Awareness
- Learn basic greetings in local language
- Understand local dining customs and meal times
- Research appropriate dress codes for religious sites
- Be aware of local holidays that might affect plans

## Transportation Tips
- Research public transportation options
- Consider walkability of neighborhoods when choosing accommodations
- Download offline maps and translation apps
- Understand local taxi/rideshare culture
